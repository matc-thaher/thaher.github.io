My Research Areas

Dark Matter

My research focuses on investigating the nature of Dark Matter, a mysterious substance that makes up approximately 85% of the universe’s mass. I explore various theoretical models to understand its properties and potential interactions with known particles.

Gravitational Wave Astronomy

I have worked on signal processing techniques for analyzing data from gravitational wave detectors, focusing on methods for glitch detection and removal using adaptive spline fitting and wavelet shrinkage.

Big Data

Incorporating machine learning techniques in physics research has been a significant aspect of my work. I develop and apply algorithms to improve the analysis of large datasets, particularly in astrophysical phenomena.
