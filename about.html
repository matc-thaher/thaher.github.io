Hello, I am Mohammad Abu Thaher Chowdhury. I am currently a PhD student at the RPI in Troy, New York, USA, working as a graduate research assistant with Professor Victor Robles Sanchez. I completed my Masters in 2022 at the University of Texas Rio Grande Valley, supervised by Professor Soumya D Mohanty. My thesis was entitled "Glitch Estimation and Removal Using Adaptive Spline Fitting and Wavelet Shrinkage on the Gravitational Wave Data" and is available from the ProQuest eTheses Repository.
